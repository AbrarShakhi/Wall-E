Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 11th, 2025

Welcome to Wall-E, a service exclusively for East West University (EWU) students. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your institutional information with the utmost care and respect for your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Institutional Data: We access basic student information through Wall-E, including:
    • Student ID
    • Student Name
    • Student Email (@std.ewubd.edu)
    • Advisor Email
  • Usage Information: We collect basic system logs to ensure proper functionality and security.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To facilitate automated email communication between students and advisors
  • To verify your status as an EWU student
  • To ensure system security and prevent unauthorized access

3. Data Protection & Security

  • Your institutional data remains within your device
  • We do not store passwords or sensitive academic records
  • Access is strictly limited to verified EWU students
  • We do not share your information with any third parties

4. Limited Access

Wall-E is exclusively available to East West University students. Authentication is handled through institutional Email ID, and unauthorized access attempts are prohibited.

5. Your Rights

As an EWU student, you have the right to:

  • Access your information within the system
  • Request correction of any inaccurate data
  • Report any unauthorized use of the system
  • Opt-out of automated email communications

6. Policy Updates

Any changes to this privacy policy will be communicated through your institutional email.
